# Player Guide — Joining the Campaign

Welcome! This guide walks you through everything you need to do before your first session.

---

## Step 1 — Open Your Invite Link

Your GM will send you a link that looks like:

```
https://<tunnel-url>/invite/join/<code>
```

Open it in your browser. Enter your **player name** when prompted, then click **Join Campaign**.

> This creates your account in the campaign. You only need to do this once.

---

## Step 2 — Create Your Character

After joining, you'll be redirected to your **Player Portal**. Click **Create Character** (or ask your GM for the character creation link directly):

```
https://<tunnel-url>/character-creator?playerId=<your-id>
```

> **Important:** Always use the link with `?playerId=` in it. This connects your character to your campaign slot so the GM can see it.

---

## Step 3 — Build Your Character (Manual or AI)

You'll see the character creation wizard. There are two ways to fill it out:

### Option A — Manual (Step-by-Step)

Work through each step at your own pace:

1. **Race** — Choose your race and subrace
2. **Class** — Choose your class (and subclass if applicable at level 1)
3. **Background** — Grants two skill proficiencies and starting equipment
4. **Ability Scores** — Assign the Standard Array or use Point Buy
5. **Skills** — Choose additional skill proficiencies from your class list
6. **Equipment** — Pick a starting equipment package
7. **Spells** *(if your class casts spells)* — Select cantrips and known/prepared spells
8. **Level Features** *(if level 4+)* — Choose Ability Score Improvements or Feats
9. **Details & Review** — Add your name, backstory, alignment, and assign your Foundry account

### Option B — AI Generation

Click **Generate with AI** and describe your character concept in plain English. Examples:

- *"A brooding half-elf warlock with a dark past"*
- *"A cheerful gnome wizard obsessed with fire spells"*
- *"A grizzled veteran fighter who became a city guard"*

The AI will fill in every field following PHB rules. You can then **edit any step** to adjust anything you don't like before syncing.

---

## Step 4 — Assign Your Foundry Account *(Critical)*

On the **Details & Review** step, scroll down to **Assign to Foundry Player**.

You will see a row of buttons showing the Foundry VTT player accounts:

```
[ GM Only ]  [ PlayerName1 ]  [ PlayerName2 ]  ...
```

**Select your own name.** This gives you ownership of your character actor in Foundry VTT so you can:
- Open and edit your own character sheet
- Move your token on the map
- Roll dice from your sheet

> If you skip this step your character will be owned by the GM only and you won't be able to interact with it in Foundry.

---

## Step 5 — Sync to Foundry VTT

Once all steps are complete, click **Sync to Foundry VTT** at the bottom of the review page.

You'll see a confirmation that your character was created. It will now appear in Foundry VTT under the **Actors** tab, fully populated with:

- Ability scores and saving throws
- Skill proficiencies
- Starting equipment
- Spell slots and spells (if applicable)
- Your biography and backstory

Log into Foundry VTT with the account your GM set up for you. Your character sheet will be waiting.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| "Invalid invite" error | Check the link is complete and hasn't expired — ask your GM to verify it |
| Foundry player list is empty | Your GM needs to have Foundry VTT running when you sync |
| Character synced but you can't open it in Foundry | You didn't select your name in Step 4 — ask your GM to reassign ownership |
| Page won't load | The GM may have restarted the tunnel — ask for the new URL |
